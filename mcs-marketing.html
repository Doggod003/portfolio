MCS Marketing Collateral System

Python-based PDF generation pipeline for scaled healthcare marketing

The problem: PrimeCare Medical needed recurring, high-quality marketing materials—recruiting packets, conference collateral, B2B outreach to county jail administrators. Each custom-designed multi-page packet took time in InDesign or Canva and didn't scale across repeated use cases. Manual design work had high turnaround and couldn't keep pace with multiple simultaneous campaigns.

The solution

Built MCS—a Python-based PDF generation pipeline that produces marketing packets at scale. The system uses Python and ReportLab to programmatically generate multi-page PDFs with consistent branding, variable content, and precise formatting. Templates are defined once, then reused and customized for different campaigns without manual redesign.

Core capability: Define a marketing packet template once (layout, typography, brand assets), then generate hundreds of variations by swapping copy, images, and targeting data. No design tool required for subsequent campaigns.

What it produced

The system generated three major campaign suites:

  • Nurses Week (May 2026) — Recognition posters, magnets, gift tags, appreciation cards, booth flyers, sticker sheets for recognition events
  • NCCHC Conference 2026 — Booth collateral and candidate-facing recruiting content, with companion React landing page for digital sign-ups
  • County Jail Administrator B2B Packet — Sales/outreach collateral targeting decision-makers in correctional facilities
